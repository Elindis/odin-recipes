Mashed potatoes

Description

This mashed potatoes is a recipe from my youth. It uses potatoes, butter, and seasoning to create a fast, simple childhood favourite.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • butter
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil potato
  2. Drain pot
  3. Add butter, salt, and pepper
  4. Mash vigorously
  5. Serve
