Beef stroganoff

Description

This beef stroganoff is a recipe from my youth. It uses ground beef, mushroom soup, noodles, and seasoning to create a fast, simple childhood favourite.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Mushroom soup
  • Noodles

Instructions

  1. Cook beef
  2. Add soup
  3. Simmer
  4. Cook noodles
  5. Combine
  6. Serve
